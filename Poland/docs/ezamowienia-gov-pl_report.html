Poland - e-Zamówienia

  • Access: Strong public access through the BZP site and public notice APIs.
  • Medical data: General procurement platform, but very useful for medical tenders because notices can be filtered and parsed by subject and lot text.
  • Detail level: Very strong notice data; lot information is public but usually embedded in HTML rather than a clean lot table.
  • Feasibility: Medical or healthcare extraction is feasible, and tender or lot-level extraction is feasible; item-level extraction is moderately feasible but not always normalized into a clean table.
  • Best count evidence: 322,175 strict published notices, 338,058 award/result notices, and 591,135 contract-performance notices.
