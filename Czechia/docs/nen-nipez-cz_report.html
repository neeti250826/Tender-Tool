Czechia - nen.nipez.cz

  • Access: Public and readable without login.
  • Medical data: General procurement portal, not medical-only, but usable for medical tenders where contracting authorities publish healthcare purchases.
  • Detail level: Strong public detail with subject-matter items, CPV/NIPEZ codes, publication history, and lot signals.
  • Feasibility: Tender and item or lot-level extraction is feasible, and medical or healthcare extraction is feasible using codes, item text, and contracting-authority filters.
  • Count status: Exact 2024+ counting was not finalized, but the portal is a strong extraction target.
