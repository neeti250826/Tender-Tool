Hungary - EKR

  • Access: Public with open notice APIs.
  • Medical data: General procurement portal, but well-suited to finding medical tenders through notice text, authority name, and downloaded PDFs.
  • Detail level: Strong notice-level structure; lot or item detail is usually easier to recover from notice PDFs or TED links.
  • Feasibility: Medical or healthcare extraction is feasible, and tender-level extraction is feasible; item-level extraction is only partially feasible because much of the depth sits in PDFs or TED documents.
  • Best count evidence: 54,294 published notices and 21,300 strict award/result notices from 2024-01-01 to 2026-06-03.
