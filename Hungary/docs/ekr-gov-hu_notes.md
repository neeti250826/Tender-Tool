# Hungary - ekr.gov.hu

- Portal: `https://ekr.gov.hu/portal/kozbeszerzes/hirdetmenyek`
- Access: Public pages are reachable without VPN or login.
- Public granularity: Tender/procedure-level details are public. Public detail pages expose notice history and downloadable PDFs; item/lot detail appears to be mostly embedded in documents or deeper procedure details rather than a clean public list field.
- 2024+ awarded/complete/published count: Exact count was not publicly exposed in an easy aggregate endpoint during this pass.
- Tender-item extraction feasibility: `Medium` through public detail pages and downloaded notice/PDF parsing; `Low` for clean structured item extraction from the listing alone.
- Notes: Strong public transparency for notice history, but structured counting and item extraction likely need PDF/document parsing.
