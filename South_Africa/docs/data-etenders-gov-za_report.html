South Africa - data.etenders.gov.za

  • Access: Public with OCDS API and downloads.
  • Medical data: Medical tenders are publicly identifiable through titles, descriptions, and healthcare-related attachments on the official eTenders portal.
  • Detail level: The OCDS API schema includes item fields such as description, quantity, and unit, but sampled live releases relied more on attached documents than on populated tender.items.
  • Feasibility: Feasible because tender data is public, medical tenders are public, and item name, unit, and quantity are extractable from official tender PDFs even when the structured API is sparse.
  • Evidence note: Official public PDFs include examples such as Alcohol Swabs, Per 200, and Approximate Quantity 2000, plus other healthcare pricing tables with explicit quantities.
